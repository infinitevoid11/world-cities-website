About Dubai

Dubai is a city and emirate in the United Arab Emirates known for luxury shopping, ultramodern architecture, and a lively nightlife scene. Burj Khalifa, an 830m-tall tower, dominates the skyscraper-filled skyline.

Once a small fishing village, Dubai has transformed into one of the world's most glamorous, modern, and progressive cities. The city has become iconic for its skyscrapers and high-rise buildings, including the world's tallest building, the Burj Khalifa.
