About New York

New York City comprises 5 boroughs sitting where the Hudson River meets the Atlantic Ocean. At its core is Manhattan, a densely populated borough that's among the world's major commercial, financial, and cultural centers.

Home to the headquarters of the United Nations, New York is an important center for international diplomacy. The city is a major global hub of business and commerce and is sometimes described as the capital of the world.
